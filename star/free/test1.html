About ConnectBots

Project Overview: ConnectBots is an innovative platform aimed at revolutionizing the delivery, payment, and transportation systems in Botswana. We are building an all-in-one web application that connects users with local stores, offers a unified payment system, facilitates e-commerce for local products like beef and diamonds, manages transportation routes, and provides job opportunities for vehicle owners. Our mission is to enhance convenience, efficiency, and economic opportunities for the people of Botswana.
